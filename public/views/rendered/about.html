My name is Brandon Roth. I'm a web developer based in Atlanta, Georgia, where I've lived for nearly 20 years. I received a Bachelor of Arts from the Univeristy of Georgia Honors College. To me, this means using a build process like gulp, testing with Mocha, Chai, Karma, etc., linting with ESLint, version control with git, and using functional programming design for efficiency and maintanability. In my spare time, I enjoy flying around the north Georgia mountains, spending time with friends and family, traveling, and reading.
